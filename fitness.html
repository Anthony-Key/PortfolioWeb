The Beginning - Summer 2021

This was a pivotal moment in my life and an eye opener. When seeing myself in such a state something had to change. I started by going to a gym, thinking this would solve a lot of my issues however, losing weight isn't just fitness its dieting as well probably 80 dieting 20 fitness. After realising I needed to sort my diet out, I had a high intake of sugar that needed to slow down. Rather then cutting it completely I incremently decreased my sugar consumption as cutting it instantly would put me off the dieting so doing this incremently helped me manage myself.
