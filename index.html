Portfolio

Unity Projects

Over the years I've worked on many different projects and as a result grown substantially. I started Unity at college in 2016, playing with the tools and trying to build my first racing game. Since then I've always had a passion in creating games and applications for others to try out and get feedback from. I then did my dissertation in Unity and achieved a 80 grade (first) for an educational game that accelerated my passion for Unity and after went straight into a job with VRSS as a graduation & Junior Game Developer. After a year, I was promoted to Mid-level Game developer and now on a journey for Senior.

ASP.NET Projects

Throughout the years of working commercially, I have spent some time on personal projects and small portions at work looking at ASP.Net. On my github there are a few repositorys of my attempts at Web API's and Web app. Ulitmately my end goal was to have a Web api that unity and my web app could connect too that would post and get data.

Hardware Projects

Hardware has always spiked an interest enabling realworld activity through code. I've only touched the surface for the projects I've build and in the future I want to dive deeper into AI robitics related projects!

Fitness

During the COVID-19 lockdown, many people found themselves confined to their homes, leading to a sedentary lifestyle. Unfortunately, I was no exception and my weight ballooned from 85kg to 112kg. However, I made a conscious decision to embark on a journey with a personal trainer to shed the extra pounds and regain my physical fitness. I'm thrilled to report that I've lost a total of 20kg, bringing my weight down to a healthy 90kg. Not only has this achievement brought me great personal satisfaction, but it has also positively impacted my work life, as I feel more confident and energized.
